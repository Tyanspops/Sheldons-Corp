GUIDEBOOK


This game is based off of the real life game, "Monopoly". In this game there are two players, Player 1 and Player 2, the game starts off with the first move belonging to Player 1. From there on the moves will alternate between Player 1 and 2 after each Player has made their respective move. Moves must include rolling the dice, while there is an optional move to purchase land. Purchasing land requires the Player to have enough money to buy the land or else it is not permitted. The game ends once all plots have been purchased, or a Player has gone bankrupt. The Winner will be the Player that has the most amount of purchased land, or the Player that is not bankrupt. Goodluck and Have Fun!
